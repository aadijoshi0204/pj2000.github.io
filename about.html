I am a registered architect and IGBC Accredited Professional based in Maharashtra, currently working as a Junior Architect and Medical Planner at Language.Architecture.Body in Mumbai. My practice spans architectural design, interior design, and green building — with a particular interest in how buildings can be both deeply responsive to their environment and genuinely built to last.

"Architecture should speak of its time and place, but yearn for timelessness."

My path into practice has been shaped by varied internships — from green building certification work at Sassan Gir forest to competition design with established Mumbai practices — alongside active involvement in NASA India, where I served on the executive board as Unit Secretary. I believe the early years of a career are for breadth: absorbing as many ways of working and thinking as possible before the work begins to find its own direction.
